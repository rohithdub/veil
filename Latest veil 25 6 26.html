Why VEIL's Analysis Is Different

No black box. Every point is traceable.

Most "trust" tools give you a number and ask you to believe it. VEIL shows exactly where every point came from — and is honest about which parts are verified facts versus an AI's best read.

🔒 Verified Technical Signals Real

Computed directly from the URL and, where the registry allows it, a live RDAP lookup — HTTPS presence, domain age, suspicious TLDs, brand-impersonation patterns, and more. Deterministic, rule-based, no model involved.

🧠 AI Behavioral Read AI

Claude reasons over the verified signals plus general knowledge of the domain or brand, where it recognizes one, to estimate manipulation patterns. It's explicitly instructed to stay neutral rather than invent confident answers when it has little to go on.

📊 Confidence, stated honestly

VEIL's confidence score reflects exactly what was gathered for that specific scan — how many real signals fired, whether the live registry lookup succeeded, and whether the AI read came through. No fixed "28 signals" claim; it changes scan to scan.
